Step 3: Create a Bootable flash drive


1. After plugging your flash drive , select your device name under "Device" drop-down option.




2. Click "START" to begin partitioning, choose "Write in ISO Image mode" then OK, click "Yes"
to download necessary installation files, and then click "OK" to format your flash drive.

(WARNING: THIS WILL DELETE ALL THE FILES AND DATA IN YOUR FLASH DRIVE,
MAKE SURE TO CREATE A BACKUP BEFORE PROCEEDING!)


3. After partitioning, keep your flash drive plugged in, and simply Restart your computer.
